About Us

Welcome to KANJIRAPPUZHA DAM

Kanjirappuzha Dam is situated in Palakkad district in the state of Kerala, India. It is built across the Kanjirappuzha River, a tributary of the Bharathapuzha River. The dam primarily serves the purpose of irrigation and water supply to the surrounding areas.

It creates a large reservoir that supports agriculture in the region. Additionally, the picturesque surroundings of the dam make it a popular spot for visitors seeking natural beauty

The dam serves as a reservoir for irrigation purposes and plays an important role in supporting the agricultural needs of the region. The dam's serene surroundings, with lush green hills and picturesque landscapes, make it a popular spot for visitors.
